What is an Aupair?

Well, if you ask me, an aupair is a mix of teacher, nurse, big sister, cook, driver and mentor.
To be completely honest being an aupair is HARD, not only because taking care of kids per se is hard, but all the responsibilities that the title carries.
Through this blog i will share my experience being a childcare provider, tricks and reasources.
Happy Reading!
